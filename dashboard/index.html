HACCP Quick Reference

Critical Temperature Limits

  • Cold Holding: ≤41°F (refrigerated products)
  • Danger Zone: 41°F - 135°F (bacteria growth)
  • Critical Violation: >45°F (accelerated spoilage)
  • Time Limit: Max 2 hours above 41°F

Corrective Actions

  • 41-45°F: Monitor closely, reduce temp immediately
  • >45°F for <2hrs: Cool rapidly, document action
  • >45°F for >2hrs: Discard product, notify supervisor
  • Equipment failure: Contact maintenance immediately

Emergency Contact: Food Safety Manager — Call immediately for violations >45°F or equipment failure
